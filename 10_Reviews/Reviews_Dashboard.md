## AI Instructions

During long-term reviews:

- Identify recurring weaknesses.
- Identify recurring strengths.
- Recommend improvements to the learning strategy.
- Recommend updates to AI Architect OS only when a genuine pattern exists.
- Do not redesign the OS because of one bad week or one bad month.

Only long-term evidence should influence architectural decisions.
