# Research Roadmap

The purpose of this roadmap is to tell the AI Manager when research should become part of my learning.

Research should progress in stages.

---

## Stage 1 — Learn

Goal:

Build strong fundamentals.

Focus:

- Mathematics
- Programming
- DSA
- Computer Science
- AI

Research Activity:

❌ None

The AI should NOT assign research.

---

## Stage 2 — Read

Goal:

Become comfortable reading research papers.

Focus:

- Survey Papers
- Classic Papers
- Modern AI Papers

Research Activity:

- Paper Reading
- Paper Summaries
- Literature Review

---

## Stage 3 — Reproduce

Goal:

Reproduce published work.

Focus:

- Reimplement papers
- Verify results
- Compare implementations

Research Activity:

- Paper Reproduction
- Benchmark Testing

---

## Stage 4 — Improve

Goal:

Modify existing research.

Focus:

- Improve architectures
- Better datasets
- Better training

Research Activity:

- Experimental Research

---

## Stage 5 — Create

Goal:

Generate original ideas.

Focus:

- Novel AI Systems
- New Algorithms
- Startup Ideas

Research Activity:

- Independent Research
- Publication

---

## AI Instructions

Always determine my current stage before assigning research.

Never skip stages.

Research should only be introduced when the required curriculum has been completed.
