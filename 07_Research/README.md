# Research

## Purpose

Manage all research activities inside AI Architect OS.

This folder does NOT teach research.

The curriculum teaches research.

This folder manages research execution.

Its purpose is to help the AI Technical Manager decide:

- When research should begin.
- What research should be done.
- What research is currently active.
- What should be postponed.
- What should be completed next.

The Research Roadmap defines the progression.

The Research Dashboard tracks current work.
