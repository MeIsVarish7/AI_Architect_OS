# Rule of Three

At any point in time, I actively learn only THREE things.

---

## Main Track (70%)

The primary skill for the current month.

---

## Support Track (20%)

A skill that directly helps the Main Track.

---

## Maintenance Track (10%)

Revision and retention of previously learned skills.

---

## Rules

- Never have more than three active learning tracks.
- Finish by mastery, not by date.
- Every week must produce evidence.
- Every month must produce one major outcome.
- Every day must end with an output.
