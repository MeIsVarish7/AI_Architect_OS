# Communication

## Objective

Develop the ability to communicate technical ideas clearly through writing, speaking, documentation, collaboration, and leadership.

---

## Roadmap

Level 1 - Technical Writing

Level 2 - Documentation

Level 3 - Technical Speaking & Presentation

Level 4 - Team Collaboration

Level 5 - Professional Communication

Level 6 - Leadership & Mentoring

Level 7 - Public Presence & Personal Brand

Level 8 - Executive & Strategic Communication

---

## Exit Criteria

* Communicate technical concepts clearly.
* Write professional documentation.
* Present confidently.
* Collaborate effectively.
* Lead technical discussions.
